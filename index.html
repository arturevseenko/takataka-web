About

"A movement of the modern underground music scene — microhouse & minimal waves."

TakaTaka is an independent music label and collective based in Saint Petersburg, Russia. Founded as a platform for the deepest sounds of the underground electronic spectrum — where microhouse meets minimal wave, where rhythm becomes ritual.

The TakaTaka Podcast series gathers the best sets of talented DJs and musicians from across the underground community, presenting each episode as a close look at the intentions and ideas that drive this movement.

We operate at the intersection of digital and physical — releasing music on vinyl and digital platforms, building community around the sound, and maintaining a commitment to artistic integrity.
